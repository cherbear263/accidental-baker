Hi, I'm Cheryl.

I'm a science teacher in Bundaberg, Queensland. When I get stressed I tend to bake. I love following all the greats and trying to recreate some of their desserts. My favorites at the moment are Cedric Grolet, Adriano Zumbo, and absolute Top Pick of All is Bruno Albouze (*swoon!*) Recently I have completed a buttercream flowers course in Thailand, so I love trying to make flowers of all varieties and colours.

Logically, since I'm a teacher, I stress-bake a LOT. So, if you would like to make an order, please get in contact!
